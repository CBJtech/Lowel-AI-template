LOWEL AI Research Project 2023

LOWEL AI is an AI device that helps to detect harmful bacteria and particles in water with the use of neural networks, microscopic cameras and IoT devices. It is currently a research been conducted by a Gambian high schooler called Cherno Basiru Jallow otherwise nickname CBJ. It's main goal is to minimize diseases such as cholera, diahrrea, dysentry cause as a result of water contamination. This also involves the use of an IoT device that continously monitor water quality in a water source or container for dangerous bacteria and harmful particles.

Learn More

LOWEL AI FEATURES:

Must Include:
  • An AI device that can be used in tanks to detect and clean harmful bacteria.
  • Uses microscopic cameras to detect bacteria and other dangerous particles.
  • IOT device that can continously monitor water quality in containers and water sources.
  • A prototype that can use AI to monitor water quality.
  • Predicting water quality using machine learning and Algorithm.
  • The use of programming languages such as python.

Lowel AI aim to acheive:

  • Improve drinking water quality standards.
  • Locate and track contaminated water sources.
  • minimize infections cause by drinking contaminated water.
  • Study and analyse the growth of harmful organisms in water
  • Minimize the mortalty rate of children under the age of 5 that die due to diarrhea.
  • Use technology resources and devices to solve global crisis.
